Future Career Plan In Linux and Open Source

27 November, 2019 2:00 pm - 4:00 pm IU Auditorim

Future networking trends include the widespread adoption of 5G technology, enabling faster speeds and facilitating applications like autonomous vehicles and smart cities. The Internet of Things (IoT) will connect billions of devices, requiring efficient networking infrastructure and technologies like edge computing. Software-Defined Networking (SDN) and Network Function Virtualization (NFV) will bring flexibility and programmability to networks, while AI and machine learning will optimize network management. Quantum networking holds promise for secure communication, and network security measures will become increasingly important in a complex and interconnected world.
